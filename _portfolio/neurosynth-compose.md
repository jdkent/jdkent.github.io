Neurosynth Compose is a web-based platform for creating, reviewing, and sharing neuroimaging meta-analyses with clear provenance.
It supports researcher-in-the-loop study curation and annotation (PRISMA-aligned workflows), and connects to standardized model specifications executed via the NiMARE ecosystem.

- Platform: [compose.neurosynth.org](https://compose.neurosynth.org/)
- Paper (Imaging Neuroscience, 2026): [Neurosynth Compose: A web-based platform for flexible and reproducible neuroimaging meta-analysis](https://doi.org/10.1162/IMAG.a.1114)
